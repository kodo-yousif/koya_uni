Fuclty of Engineering

The Faculty of Engineering (FENG) at Koya University is an excellent place to acquire education. It is the center of excellence for advanced education and research in different fields of engineering. It comprised of seven departments: Civil Engineering Department, Geotechnical Engineering Department, Architectural Engineering Department; Petroleum Engineering Department, Chemical Engineering Department, Manufacturing and Industrial Engineering Department and Software Engineering Department. Moreover, Faculty of Engineering has Scientific Research Center, Engineering Consultancy Bureau, Scientific and Postgraduate Affairs Unit (SPA), Curriculum Development Unit, Quality Assurance Unit, Laboratories Unit, IT Unit, Health & Safety Unit, Accounting Unit and Workshop Unit.

Departments of FENG

  • Architectural Engineering (DARE)
  • Civil Engineering (DCEN)
  • Geotechnical Engineering (DGEN)
  • Chemical Engineering (DCHE)
  • Petroleum Engineering (DPTE)
  • Software Engineering (DSWE)
  • Manufacturing and Industrial Engineering (DMFE)

Units of the Faculty of Engineering:

  • Scientific and Postgraduate Affairs (SPA) Unit
  • Curriculum Development Unit
  • IT Unit
  • Quality Assurance Unit
  • Laboratories Unit
  • Health and Safety Unit
  • Accounting Unit
